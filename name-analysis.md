## Final Recommendation: Top 5

### 1. **The Reframe** ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- **Domain:** thereframe.com, reframe.email, reframe.so
- **Why:** Direct tie to your book "Ender's Reframe." Works perfectly standalone—"reframe how you think about AI." Professional, memorable, short. The word "reframe" is the core message of your book.
- **Tagline:** "Reframe your relationship with AI. Weekly."

### 2. **The Ansible**
- **Domain:** theansible.com, ansible.email, ansibleweekly.com
- **Why:** Iconic Ender reference. Known to sci-fi fans, sounds techy to everyone else. Means "instant communication"—perfect for a newsletter about AI.
- **Tagline:** "Instant intelligence. Every Thursday."
- **Risk:** RedHat's Ansible automation tool (but different audience)

### 3. **Third Mind**
- **Domain:** thirdmind.com, thirdmind.io, thirdmindweekly.com
- **Why:** Human mind + AI = Third Mind. Evocative, philosophical, unique. Subtle reference to "Third Invasion."
- **Tagline:** "Where human thinking meets AI."

### 4. **Foreign Signal**
- **Domain:** foreignsignal.com, foreignsignal.io
- **Why:** Ties to "The Foreign Mind." A "signal" from an intelligence we're learning to understand. Works standalone.
- **Tagline:** "Signals from the AI frontier."

### 5. **Gate Down**
- **Domain:** gatedown.com, gatedown.io
- **Why:** Direct reference to "The enemy's gate is down"—the perspective shift that wins battles. Deep cut for fans, intriguing for everyone.
- **Tagline:** "See AI differently."
- **Risk:** Might sound negative (gate is down = broken) to non-fans

## Why "The Reframe" Wins

1. **Direct book tie-in:** "Ender's Reframe" → "The Reframe"
2. **Standalone power:** "Reframe your thinking" is universally understood
3. **Action-oriented:** Implies transformation (what readers want)
4. **Professional:** Works for consultant/enterprise audience
5. **Memorable:** Single powerful word
6. **Versatile:** Works for AI content, leadership content, productivity content
7. **Future-proof:** If you expand beyond AI, the name still works

**The Reframe** lets you say: "I write The Reframe newsletter" and "My book is about Ender's Reframe for AI Leadership"—they reinforce each other.
